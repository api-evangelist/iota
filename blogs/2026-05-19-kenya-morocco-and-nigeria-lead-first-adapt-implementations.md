Kenya, Morocco, and Nigeria are the first countries to implement ADAPT. Led by the AfCFTA Secretariat and developed with the Tony Blair Institute for Global Change, the World Economic Forum, and the IOTA Foundation, the initiative is building a shared digital infrastructure for intra-African trade.
